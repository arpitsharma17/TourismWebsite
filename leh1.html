THE GRAND DRAGON LADAKH

Overlooking Stok Mountain, this grand, curved hotel is 2.4 km from the 17th-century Leh Palace. The airy, refined rooms offer free Wi-Fi, 102-cm flat-screen TVs, minifridges, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Many have mountain views and some have balconies. Suites include separate living rooms, while upgraded suites add massage chairs. Room service is offered. Free breakfast is served in a relaxed cafe featuring local decor. There's also a Tibetan/Chinese restaurant. Other amenities include a rooftop terrace and a garden with seating, plus a game room, a fitness centre, a sauna and a steam room. Parking is available.
