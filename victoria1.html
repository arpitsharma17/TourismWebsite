THE HINDUSTHAN INTERNATIONAL

2 km from Alipore Zoo, the Hindustan International features a chic nightclub, salon and the multi cuisine Oriental Express. Location:Barely 5 km from Victoria Memorial (museum), Hindustan International is centrally located in the enigmatic city of Kolkata. The hotel is 3 km from Zoological Gardens and 9 km from Behala Airport. The hotel is good for tourists as well as business travelers. Room amenities:The rooms are classified into suites, colony premium, colony, imperial and presidential suite. The hotel rooms offer 40-inch LED television, in room business center with printer, stationary kit at the work desk, choice of newspaper, bottled mineral water, minibar, hair dryer, digital safe, tea/coffee maker, DVD player and in house DVD library. Hotel amenities:There is a swimming pool, night club, fitness center, beauty parlor, boutique shop and a barber shop in the hotel. Other facilities offered by the hotel are Wi-Fi connectivity, in-house laundry service, doctor on call, separate work desk with enhanced lighting, valet car park, 24-hour currency exchange. The hotel has banquet halls to host social and business events. Dining:The hotel offers multiple dining options. The Oriental Express is a multi- cuisine restaurant with perfect oriental architecture and ambiance. There is a pastry shop, serving tasty snacks and patisseries to hungry patrons
