PARAKKAT NATURE RESORTS

Designed in harmony with the scenic countryside, Parakat Nature Resort features a restaurant and a coffee shop with stunning views. Parakat Nature Resort is located in the most scenic location in Munnar, with amazing views of the surrounding countryside. This 5 star resort is in close proximity to Dream Land Spices Park (6 km), Fun Forest Adventure Park (6 km) and Pothamedu Viewpoint (7 km). The resort is 7 km from Munnar Bus Stand, 102 km from Cochin International Airport, and 70 km from Bodinayakanur Railway Station.
