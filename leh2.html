THE ZEN LADAKH RESORT

With views of the Stok Kangri mountain range, this unpretentious hotel is 2 km from the Leh Palace monastery and 3 km from Kushok Bakula Rimpochhe Airport. Featuring mountain views, the streamlined rooms and cottages provide free Wi-FI, flat-screen TVs, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add living rooms and whirlpool baths; an upgraded suite has a garden and a kitchen. Room service is available. Continental breakfast and parking are complimentary. There's an international restaurant and a cafe, plus a health club with an indoor pool, a gym and a beauty parlour. A souvenir shop and conference rooms are also offered.
