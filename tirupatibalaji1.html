RAJ PARK

Hotel Raj Park is a 3 star property, located at 19.2 kms from Tirupati Airport, 3.8 kms from Tirupati Railway Station and 3.4 kms from Tirupati Central Bus Station. Hotel Raj Park renders comfortable and ergonomic rooms equipped with amenities like air-conditioner, cable television, intercom facility, tea/coffee maker, writing desk and well-appointed bathrooms. The property offers facilities such as business centre, conference hall, internet facility, room service, transfers and parking facility. In-house restaurant The Prime serves a wide array of scrumptious vegetarian cuisines. Renaissance offers delectable and enticing non-vegetarian delicacies. The hotel is also an ideal place for those who come for sightseeing purposes as the hotel is very near to many sightseeing places that include Kapilatheertham (400 metres), ISKCON Tirupati (1.5 kms), Sri Sri Sri Raja Rajeswari Ammavari Temple (3.9 kms), Govidha Raja Swamy Temple (4 kms) and Tirumala Venkateswara Swamy Temple (20.2 kms). Have a Wonderful stay at Hotel Raj Park
