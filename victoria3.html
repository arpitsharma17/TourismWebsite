HOTEL NIHARIKA

7 km from The Howrah Bridge, 2 km from Birla Planetarium, In-house multi-cuisine restaurant Located 1 kilometre from Rabindra Sadan Metro Station, Hotel Niharika features comfortable and decent rooms equipped with amenities such as air-conditioner, satellite television, intercom facility, tea/coffee maker, sofa unit, study table and well-appointed bathrooms. Snack basket, fruit basket, mini-bar and DVD player are chargeable. The hotel is in close proximity to Victoria Memorial Hall (2.5 km), Eden Gardens (5.5 km), Nicco Park (9.7 km), National Library (3.3 km) and Kolkata International Airport (17 km) and offers facilities such as business centre, conference hall, internet facility, 24-hour room service, central AC and parking facility. Taxi service, transfers, guide service, sightseeing and doctor on call are chargeable. In-house restaurant serves Indian, Chinese and Continental cuisines. The hotel is preferred for its centre location and amazing hospitality.
