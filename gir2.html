THE FERN GIR FOREST RESORT

Overlooking the Hiran River, this tranquil resort is 2 km from Sasan Gir train station and 11 km from Devalia Safari Park. Featuring patios, the down-to-earth suites and tents (inspired by royal Indian field tents) come with flat-screen TVs and minifridges, plus tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded suites offer river views and living areas; villas have private gardens. Room service is offered. Breakfast is complimentary. There are 3 eateries, including a fine-dining Italian restaurant with a terrace overlooking the river. Other amenities include a spa, a gym and an outdoor pool with a bar, as well as an amphitheater offering live music.
