HOTEL AGC

Hotel AGC, or Aurangabad Gymkhana Club, enjoys a prime location in the city at a distance of merely 2 km from the airport. The railway station and central bus station are also less than a 15-minute drive away from here. Surrounded by 2.5 acres of lush greenery, this unique club-cum-hotel caters to both business and leisure travellers owing to its proximity to prominent landmarks like MIDC Industrial Area and Cannought Garden (2 km). Attractions like Siddharth Garden (8.6 km), Bibi Ka Maqbara (9.7 km) and Aurangabad Caves (13 km) are also easily accessible.
