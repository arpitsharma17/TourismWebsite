THE GATEWAY HOTEL GIR FOREST

Nestled in the woods, this modern hotel is a 10-minute walk from Sasan Gir railway station and 16 km from Kamleshwar Dam. Featuring contemporary furnishings and balconies, the airy rooms have free Wi-Fi, flat-screens TVs, minibars and tea/coffeemaking equipment. Suites add separate living areas. Breakfast is free. Other amenities include an international restaurant, an exercise room and an outdoor pool with a dining area. There's also a spa area, an event space, a library and a game room.
