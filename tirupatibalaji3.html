PAI VICEROY

Proximity to the famous Kapila Theertham (400 m) and ISKCON Temple (1.4 km) makes this hotel a good choice for spiritual travellers. Tirupati Railway Station is only 3.6 km away. The hotel offers facilities like 24-hour concierge services, doctor on call, parking space, gift shop, car rental and business centre. Multi-function halls are also available here for hosting private gatherings and seminars. Book your stay in well-lit rooms and suites with stunning views of mountains. Rooms are well-equipped with modern amenities like cable television, telephone, electronic safe, wardrobe and Wi-Fi. Enjoy a variety of Indian, Chinese and Continental delicacies at Gufha restaurant-cum-bar. Plantain Leaf offers a selection of vegetarian delights. Guests can visit Sri Venkateswara Zoological Park (7.2 km) to spot white tigers and enjoy deer/lion safaris. The ancient Sri Govindaraja Swamy Temple is 3.5 km from the hotel
