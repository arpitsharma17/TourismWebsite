Location: True to its name, the property is situated in a serene, lush green environment outside the busy city. It is located in the scenic village of Chinnakanal, 18 km away from Munnar. The Munnar Terrace Greens is a 45 min drive from the Munnar Bus Stand and the closest airport is the Cochin International Airport which is 120 km away. Room Amenities: The range of spacious rooms and suites offered by the hotel come with views full of greenery and ample natural lighting through the large windows. The air-conditioned rooms offer coffee/tea makers, TV with cable connection and en-suite bathrooms. Hotel Facilities: As a resort that offers a perfect escape from the hustle bustle of routine life, this hotel offers some of the best facilities to deliver a pleasant stay for the entire family. There is a play area for kids, clubhouse with indoor sports, outdoor fields for badminton and cricket, video game consoles, battery cars and rifle shooting. Hobby workshops are also conducted. Other facilities include an internet cafe, gift shop and conference room. Dining: Given its remote location, It isn't easy to find a restaurant in close proximity the hotel. To overcome this, the in-house restaurant offers a wide menu of delicious cuisine, including Indian, Chinese and Continental.
