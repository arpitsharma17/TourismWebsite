TRIDENT HOTEL

Across the street from Back Bay, this upmarket hotel in a high-rise building is 2 km from Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya, a grand art and history museum, and 22 km from Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport. Warm rooms have flat-screens, free Wi-Fi and minibars. Upgraded rooms add sitting areas and/or bay views. Club rooms offer 1-way airport transfers, and lounge access with complimentary breakfast and cocktails. The 1- to 2-bedroom suites come with living rooms. Room service is available 24/7. Amenities include polished Mediterranean and Pan-Asian restaurants, as well as a chic cocktail bar, an outdoor pool and a spa.
