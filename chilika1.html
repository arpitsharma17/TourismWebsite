HOTEL GOLDEN DUST

Hotel Golden Dust is right on the beach,on Marine Drive Road with luxurious Super Deluxe rooms that has a great sea-view and all rooms have the luxury of enjoying the sea from their balconies.The hotel provides you with the serene beauty of the sea away from the common din. Lawn in the hotel is like an icing on the cake where you can sit and enjoy yourselves while your little ones play around and what more, you can cherish the wonderful tune of the sea breeze and relish in the rooftop cafeteria.Hotel Golden Dust is located at Baliapanda on New Marine Drive road. Govardhan Peth (Approx. 2Km) and Mata Math (Approx. 0.5 Km) are some of the hotspots in its vicinity. Puri Beach like other beaches of Puri is peaceful and extended for a relaxing walk by the sea. Numerous stalls line up around the beach for tourists to pick up souvenirs made of shells, jute and lamp shades or wall hangings made from patch work. Lakshmi temple, Markandesvara temple and Vimala temple is also worth-exploring.
